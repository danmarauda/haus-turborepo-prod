# First Home Buyer Guide - Queensland (QLD)

## Overview

Queensland offers one of Australia's most generous First Home Owner Grants at **$30,000**, plus significant stamp duty concessions. The state's affordable housing market makes it attractive for first home buyers.

## First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)

### Grant Amount
- **$30,000** for new homes (one of the highest in Australia!)
- Property value must be **less than $750,000**

### What Qualifies as a "New Home"
- Newly constructed house, unit, or townhouse
- Off-the-plan purchase (never lived in)
- Substantially renovated home
- Home built by owner-builder

### Eligibility Requirements
1. Australian citizen or permanent resident
2. At least 18 years old
3. You (and spouse) have never owned property in Australia
4. Must live in the property as your home for 6 continuous months
5. Must move in within 1 year of completion/settlement

## Stamp Duty (Transfer Duty) Concessions

### First Home Concession
| Property Value | Concession |
|----------------|------------|
| Up to $500,000 | Full exemption ($0 duty) |
| $500,001 - $550,000 | Sliding scale reduction |
| Over $550,000 | Standard rates apply |

### First Home Vacant Land Concession
| Land Value | Concession |
|------------|------------|
| Up to $250,000 | Full exemption |
| $250,001 - $400,000 | Sliding scale reduction |

### Savings Example
| Purchase Price | Standard Duty | First Home Buyer | Savings |
|----------------|---------------|------------------|---------|
| $450,000 | $10,600 | $0 | $10,600 |
| $500,000 | $15,925 | $0 | $15,925 |
| $550,000 | $18,200 | ~$4,550 | ~$13,650 |

## Queensland Housing Finance Loan

### Key Features
- Low-deposit loans for eligible buyers
- Competitive interest rates
- No LMI required
- Income limits apply

### Eligibility
- Household income under $180,000
- First home buyer or haven't owned in 10+ years
- Property value limits apply by region

## Step-by-Step Journey

### 1. Maximize Your Benefits
- [ ] Target new homes under $750,000 for $30,000 FHOG
- [ ] Stay under $500,000 for full stamp duty exemption
- [ ] Consider regional QLD for better value

### 2. Financial Preparation
- [ ] Save 5-10% deposit
- [ ] Get pre-approval
- [ ] Factor in $30,000 grant toward deposit/costs

### 3. Property Search
- [ ] Use HAUS to find new developments
- [ ] Explore house and land packages
- [ ] Consider growth corridors (Ipswich, Logan, Moreton Bay)

### 4. Apply for Benefits
- [ ] FHOG: Apply through your lender at settlement
- [ ] Stamp duty concession: Applied automatically
- [ ] Keep all documentation for 5 years
