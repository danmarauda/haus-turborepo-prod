# First Home Buyer Guide - Western Australia (WA)

## Overview

Western Australia offers excellent value for first home buyers with affordable housing, the First Home Owner Grant, and the unique Keystart low-deposit loan program.

## First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)

### Grant Amount
- **$10,000** for new homes
- Property value must be **$750,000 or less**

### Eligibility Requirements
1. Australian citizen or permanent resident
2. At least 18 years old
3. Never owned property in Australia (you or spouse)
4. Must live in the property for 6 continuous months
5. Must move in within 12 months of settlement

## Stamp Duty (Transfer Duty) Concessions

### First Home Owner Rate
| Property Value | Duty Rate |
|----------------|-----------|
| Up to $430,000 | Exempt ($0) |
| $430,001 - $530,000 | Concessional rate |
| Over $530,000 | Standard rates |

### Savings Example

## Keystart Home Loans

### What is Keystart?
A WA Government initiative offering low-deposit home loans without Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI).

### Key Benefits
- **2% deposit** for eligible buyers
- No LMI required
- Competitive interest rates
- Shared Ownership option available

### Eligibility
- Household income limits (varies by location and household size)
- Perth Metro: ~$105,000 (single), ~$150,000 (couple)
- Regional: Higher limits apply
- Must be first home buyer or re-entering market

### Shared Ownership
- Keystart owns a portion (up to 30%)
- Lower repayments
- Buy out Keystart's share over time
