# First Home Buyer Guide - Victoria (VIC)

## Overview

Victoria offers generous support for first home buyers through stamp duty exemptions, the First Home Owner Grant, and the innovative Victorian Homebuyer Fund shared equity scheme.

## First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)

### Grant Amount
- **$10,000** for new homes in metropolitan Melbourne
- **$10,000** for new homes in regional Victoria
- Property value must be **$750,000 or less**

### Eligibility Requirements
1. Australian citizen or permanent resident
2. At least 18 years old
3. Never owned property in Australia (you or your spouse)
4. Must live in the property for 12 continuous months within the first year
5. Property must be a new home (never lived in)

## Victorian Homebuyer Fund (Shared Equity)

### How It Works
The Victorian Government contributes up to **25%** of the purchase price (or 35% for Aboriginal/Torres Strait Islander buyers), reducing your deposit and loan requirements.

### Eligibility
- Household income up to **$130,000** (singles) or **$200,000** (couples/families)
- Property value up to **$950,000** (Melbourne) or **$700,000** (regional)
- Must be an Australian citizen
- First home buyer OR previous homeowner who doesn't currently own property

### Benefits
- Lower deposit required (as low as 5%)
- No Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI)
- Smaller mortgage = lower repayments
