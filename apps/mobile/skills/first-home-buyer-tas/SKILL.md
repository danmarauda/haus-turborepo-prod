# First Home Buyer Guide - Tasmania (TAS)

## Overview

Tasmania offers Australia's **highest First Home Owner Grant at $30,000**, plus generous stamp duty exemptions and the MyHome shared equity scheme. Combined with relatively affordable housing, Tasmania is excellent for first home buyers.

## First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)

### Grant Amount
- **$30,000** for new homes (tied with QLD for highest in Australia!)
- Property value must be **$750,000 or less**

### Eligibility Requirements
1. Australian citizen or permanent resident
2. At least 18 years old
3. Never owned property in Australia (you or spouse)
4. Must live in the property for 6 continuous months
5. Must move in within 12 months of completion

## Stamp Duty Concessions

### First Home Buyer Duty Concession
| Property Value | Concession |
|----------------|------------|
| Up to $750,000 | 50% reduction on established homes |
| New homes | Additional benefits may apply |

## MyHome Shared Equity Scheme

### How It Works
The Tasmanian Government contributes up to **30%** of the purchase price, reducing your deposit and loan requirements.

### Eligibility
- Household income under **$120,000**
- Property value up to **$600,000** (Hobart) or **$500,000** (regional)
- First home buyer or previous owner who doesn't currently own
- Must be Tasmanian resident

### Benefits
- Lower deposit (as low as 2%)
- No LMI required
- Smaller mortgage = lower repayments
- Buy out government share over time
