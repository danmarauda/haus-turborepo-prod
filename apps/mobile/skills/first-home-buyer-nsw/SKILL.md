# First Home Buyer Guide - New South Wales (NSW)

## Overview

New South Wales offers significant benefits for first home buyers, including stamp duty exemptions and the First Home Owner Grant. This guide covers everything you need to know to maximize your benefits.

## First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)

### Grant Amount
- **$10,000** for new homes valued up to $600,000
- Available for newly built homes, off-the-plan purchases, or building contracts

### Eligibility Requirements
1. You must be an Australian citizen or permanent resident
2. You must be at least 18 years old
3. You (or your spouse) must not have previously owned property in Australia
4. You must live in the property as your principal place of residence for at least 6 continuous months within 12 months of settlement
5. The property must be a new home (not previously occupied)

## Stamp Duty Concessions

### Full Exemption (No Stamp Duty)
- **New homes**: Up to $800,000 purchase price
- **Vacant land**: Up to $350,000 (to build your first home)

### Concessional Rate
- **New homes**: $800,001 - $1,000,000 (sliding scale reduction)
- **Vacant land**: $350,001 - $450,000 (sliding scale reduction)

### Existing Homes (Transfer Duty)
- **Full exemption**: Up to $800,000
- **Concessional rate**: $800,001 - $1,000,000
